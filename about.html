About Us

One of NIgeria's top university

The Air Force Institute of Technology (AFIT) is a Nigerian military university established in 1976 to provide advanced technical training for officers of the Nigerian Air Force.
Located in Kaduna, We offers undergraduate and postgraduate programs in Engineering, science and technology fields relevant to the Nigerian Air Force.
The institute is renowned for its high academic standards and its role in developing skilled personnel for the Nigerian Air Force and the broader Nigerian defense sector.
